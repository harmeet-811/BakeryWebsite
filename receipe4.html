They are soft, chewy, and loaded with rich peanut flavor, perfect for satisfying sweet cravings üçû

Preparation Time

Prep Time: 15 minutes |Cook Time: 10-12 minutes | Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter (creamy or chunky)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • A pinch of salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the peanut butter, sugar, egg, vanilla extract, baking soda, and salt. Stir until smooth.
  3. Roll the dough into small balls, about 1-inch in size.
  4. Place them on a baking sheet, leaving space between each cookie.
  5. Use a fork to gently press a criss-cross pattern on top of each cookie.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden.
  7. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
