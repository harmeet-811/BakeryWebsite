🍞Choco Lava Pastry

It's is a rustic, hand-crafted bread with a crispy crust and soft, airy interior, made using traditional baking methods. 🍞

Preparation Time

Prep Time: 20 minutes |Resting Time: 8-12 hours (overnight)|Cook Time: 30-40 minutes | Total Time: 10-14 hours

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups (450g) all-purpose or bread flour
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 ½ cups (360ml) warm water

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, salt, and yeast.
  2. Add warm water and mix until a sticky dough forms.
  3. Cover with plastic wrap and let it rest at room temperature for 8-12 hours (overnight).
  4. Lightly flour a surface and turn the dough out.
  5. Gently shape it into a ball, avoiding over-handling.
  6. Place it on parchment paper and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  7. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  8. Place a Dutch oven (or oven-safe pot with a lid) inside for 30 minutes to heat up.
  9. Carefully place the dough with parchment paper into the hot Dutch oven.
  10. Cover with a lid and bake for 30 minutes.
  11. Remove the lid and bake for 10 more minutes for a golden crust.
  12. Let the bread cool on a wire rack before slicing.
  13. Enjoy your crispy, airy artisan bread! 🍞
