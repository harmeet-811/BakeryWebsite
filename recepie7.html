🍞Multigrain Bread

It is fiber-rich bread made with a blend of different grains and seeds.

Preparation Time

Prep Time: 20 minutes |Resting Time: 1.5-2 hours (overnight)|Cook Time: 30 minutes | Total Time: 2.5 hours

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ¼ cup flaxseeds (or other seeds like sunflower, chia, or sesame)
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1 ¼ cups warm water
  • 2 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 1 ½ tsp salt

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix warm water, yeast, and honey. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flours, oats, seeds, and salt. Add yeast mixture and oil. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1-1.5 hours until doubled.
  5. Punch down dough, shape it into a loaf, and place in a greased loaf pan. Let it rise for 30 minutes.
  6. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C) and bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Let it cool before slicing.
