It's light, flaky, and buttery French pastry made with laminated dough, creating delicate, crispy layers. ü•ê‚ú®

Preparation Time

Prep Time: 1 hour |Resting Time: 8-12 hours (overnight)|Cook Time: 20 mins | Total Time: 10-14 hours

Ingredients

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp (10g) instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup (240ml) warm milk
  • 2 tbsp (30g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter (cold)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp milk

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
  2. Add warm milk and softened butter, then knead until a smooth dough forms.
  3. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (or overnight).
  4. Place cold butter between two sheets of parchment paper and roll into a 7x7 inch square.
  5. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  6. Roll out the chilled dough into a 10x10 inch square.
  7. Place the butter layer in the center and fold the dough over it like an envelope.
  8. Roll it into a long rectangle, then fold it into thirds.
  9. Wrap in plastic and chill for 30 minutes.
  10. Repeat the rolling and folding process two more times, chilling between each fold.
  11. Roll out the dough into a long rectangle (~1/4-inch thick).
  12. Cut into triangles (base: 3-4 inches, height: 8 inches).
  13. Roll each triangle from base to tip to form croissants.
  14. Place on a baking sheet and let rise for 2 hours.
  15. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  16. Brush croissants with egg wash.
  17. Bake for 18-20 minutes until golden brown.
  18. Let cool slightly and enjoy! ü•ê
