Churros

They have a crispy exterior and a soft, fluffy interior.

Preparation Time

Prep Time: 15 minutes |Cook Time: 10 minutes | Total Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 ½ tablespoons sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • ½ cup sugar (for coating)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, combine water, sugar, salt, and butter. Bring to a boil.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in the flour until a dough forms. Let it cool slightly.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla extract, then mix until smooth.
  4. Transfer the dough to a piping bag with a star tip.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Pipe the dough into the hot oil, cutting with scissors. Fry until golden brown.
  7. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  8. Roll in a mixture of sugar and cinnamon. Serve warm with chocolate sauce.
